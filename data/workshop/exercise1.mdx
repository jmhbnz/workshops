Welcome to the *OpenShift automation workshop*.

In this workshop you'll explore how platform engineers can use [Ansible Automation Platform](https://www.redhat.com/en/technologies/management/ansible) to automate common operational tasks in OpenShift with re-usable, composable playbooks assembled in to end to end workflows.

Additionally this workshop will provide some foundational knowledge and experience of working with Ansible Automation Platform so OpenShift platform engineers will be prepared to support ansible users within their organisation.

**Let's get started!**


## 1.1 - The scenario

For this hands on workshop, we're going to be an OpenShift team member at **ACME Financial Services**, a large bank operating in New Zealand. They run a significant number of customer facing workloads on OpenShift and have a small core team of platform engineers supporting the platform.

![acme](/workshops/static/images/fsi-security/acme.jpg)

Throughout this workshop we'll be in the shoes of Sarah, a senior OpenShift Platform Engineer at ACME, a day in the life for her typically sees her handling platform health and maintenance tasks, assisting application teams with feature requests and she is also rostered on to the platform incident management queue on a regular rotation.
