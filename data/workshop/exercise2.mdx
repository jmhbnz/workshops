The phone buzzed angrily on the nightstand. It was 02:14 AM. Sarah, squinted at the caller ID: *ACME SOC Incident Team*.

> "Sarah, we have a problem," the voice on the other end said frantically. "Our automated anomaly detection just flagged highly irregular egress traffic coming from the `payment-gateway` namespace on the `acme-prod` cluster! We think a threat actor has breached the container, you need to get online right now!!

Sarah threw open her laptop and immediately connected to the bank's VPN, trying to keep calm and shake off sleep brain fog as a sense of panic set in...
