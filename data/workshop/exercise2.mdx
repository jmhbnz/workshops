Welcome to the *AAP + OpenShift automation workshop*.

In this workshop you'll explore how platform engineers can use Ansible Automation Platform to automate common operational tasks in OpenShift.

Rather than manually executing cluster administration tasks, you'll build reusable automation that can be triggered on demand or integrated into operational workflows.



## 1.1 - The scenario

You are a platform engineer at ACME Financial Services. The platform team is responsible for ensuring cluster reliability, security, and recoverability across multiple OpenShift environments.

One of your critical responsibilities is ensuring **etcd backups are performed regularly and stored safely off-cluster**.

Historically, this has been a bash scripted process run by engineers during maintenance windows which was cumbersome to maintain.

Your mission is to replace this manual process with an Ansible Automation Platform Job Template.


## 1.2 - What you're working with

In this environment you already have:

- A functional OpenShift cluster
- Ansible Automation Platform installed and configured
- Credentials allowing AAP to interact with the OpenShift cluster
- Access to a pre-created Automation Controller project

Your goal is to wire these components together into a repeatable automation workflow.
